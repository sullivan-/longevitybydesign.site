Services

With twenty years experience on the JVM, including over five years with Scala, we can offer you the best in hands-on Scala system design and development.  We specialize in domain modeling and database integration. That said, we are willing and able to help you with all aspects of project development.
  • We can help you establish a greenfield project using Scala, NoSQL (such as MongoDB or Cassandra), longevity, and any other technologies of your choosing.
  • We can assist with our practical experience using Lightbend tools such as Play, Akka, Akka HTTP, SBT, and Typesafe Config.
  • You can leverage well over a decade of experience in Requirements Gathering, Domain Modeling, Schema Modeling, and System Design.
  • We can help you set up or clean up your project infrastructure, such as Cloud Services, Continuous Integration, Build and Deploy Systems, Test Suites, and Code Reviews.
  • We can troubleshoot and resolve performance problems.
  • We can help your develop or refine an agile process that works for you.
  • We can mentor your engineers.
  • We can give deep-dive seminars to your team on subjects like Domain Driven Design, SBT, Scala Scripting, NoSQL Architectures, Reactive Programming, and longevity.
In our partnership with the longevity framework, we are offering substantial discounts from our usual rates for work on any project using longevity. We offer further discounts for work on open source projects, and a shared-cost approach to developing the longevity features your need.
